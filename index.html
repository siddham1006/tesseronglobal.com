Who We Are

Built by practitioners,
not theorists


Tesseron Global is a technology consulting firm founded by specialists who have spent years doing the actual work — implementing enterprise systems, leading complex change programs, and hardening organizations against cyber threats.

The name draws from the tesseract — a four-dimensional cube. Like our namesake, we operate across multiple dimensions simultaneously: technical depth, strategic clarity, execution discipline, and human adoption.

We are headquartered in Noida, India with consulting presence in the United States, serving mid-market and enterprise clients who need reliable, experienced delivery partners — not slide decks.

What We Do

Services built for
enterprise complexity

We operate across six practice areas — each staffed by specialists who have delivered in the real world.

SaaS Implementation
End-to-end implementation of enterprise SaaS platforms including Workday, MDM/PIM systems, and cloud productivity suites. Configuration, data migration, and go-live support.
Cybersecurity & DPDP Assessment
Comprehensive security audits, vulnerability assessments, and DPDP compliance reviews. We help organizations understand their risk posture and build remediation roadmaps.
Data & MDM Governance
Master Data Management strategy, data model design, governance frameworks, and product information management for global retail and enterprise clients.
Change Management
Structured change strategy, stakeholder engagement, training program design, and adoption measurement for large-scale technology transformations.
Digital Transformation
Process analysis, automation design, workflow optimization, and technology roadmapping to modernize operations and drive measurable efficiency gains.
Project & Programme Delivery
Agile and structured project management across complex, multi-stakeholder engagements. Risk management, governance setup, and reporting that keeps leadership informed.
